Rajasthan Single Sign‑On (SSO): What it is & why it matters

Rajasthan SSO provides one secure identity to access multiple government services. How SSO works, who should use it, prerequisites, and the official portals you’ll rely on.

Register / Sign In Official Portals
Single identity

Use one SSO ID to authenticate across integrated state services.

Secure access

OTP and strong-password practices improve account safety on public or shared devices.

Time‑saving

Avoid repeated registrations and move faster across services like eMitra or Recruitment.

Citizen‑first

Purpose‑built for residents, applicants, employees and kiosk operators in Rajasthan.

Who should use Rajasthan SSO?

  • Citizens accessing state services and certificates.
  • Applicants using state Recruitment and examination portals.
  • eMitra operators delivering assisted services.
  • Government employees accessing departmental systems.

Prerequisites

  • Active mobile number for OTP.
  • Personal email ID for notices and recovery.
  • Identity option (e.g., Jan Aadhaar / Aadhaar / permissible alternatives as shown on SSO portal).
  • Accurate personal details for profile verification.

Rajasthan SSO Registration & Login — Step by Step (Part 2)

Registration & Login — Step‑by‑Step

This section provides a detailed walkthrough of creating your Rajasthan SSO ID, logging in, and handling password or ID recovery.

How to Register (Citizens)

  1. Go to sso.rajasthan.gov.in and click Register.
  2. Select registration type: Citizen, UDYOG, or Government Employee.
  3. Choose identity option (Jan Aadhaar, Bhamashah, Aadhaar, or Google).
  4. Enter personal details, verify mobile via OTP, and set a secure password.
  5. Save your SSO ID and password safely for future logins.

Login Methods

  • Visit SSO Sign‑In.
  • Enter your SSO ID & password, then CAPTCHA.
  • Or use OTP login (mobile number verification).

Password & ID Recovery

  • Click Forgot Password on the sign‑in page to reset using OTP/email.
  • Click Forgot SSOID for username recovery (Aadhaar/Jan Aadhaar details required).
  • For recruitment mapping issues, use the recruitment portal helpdesk.

Next Step

Once registered, you can log in and access integrated portals like eMitra or Recruitment. Proceed to Part 3 for services and help details.
